Swiss Re start-up Academy 2020

This was an entrepreneurship camp implemented by Junior Achievement Uganda and hosted by Swiss Re-insurance company and Aiduction International. It was an interactive program to develop business plans. We were offered mentoring on topics of pitiching ideas to investors, identifying coustomer needs, solutions, unique selling points, marketing, negotiating, influencing and teamwork.

For my group, our idea was ‘Comfort Sanitary Pads’ which is a business idea of providing reusable sanitary pads for school-going children in Northern Uganda. Comfort Sanitary pads come to avail reusable sanitary pads accompanied with anti-bacterial wash with a lower annual cumulative cost. A pack of 2 pads will cost 6000 Ugandan shillings and will take them for a year compared to the current one-time disposable pads of 3000 shillings.”
